Baker:

I'm a home-based baker specializing on Filipino-flavored cakes, breads, and desserts

I started baking since 2020 in the midst of Pandemic however been loving baking and cooking since childhood. I love baking cakes because of a childhood experience.

Due to financial restrictions, my family couldn't afford to buy cakes during birthdays or any special occassions. Ever since then, I told myself I would not let my kids experience the sadness of not celebrating birthdays without cakes.

Now, my passion to bake is not only for cakes but also some special breads like ensaymada, pandesal and even sourdough.
